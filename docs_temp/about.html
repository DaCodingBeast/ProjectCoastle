About Us

COASTLE is a coastal city classification tool built to predict whether a city is coastal based on weather data.

This project was developed by two rising juniors in high school from San Diego, California — Arya Balenga and Ryan Sharifi. It was built during the summer of 2025 as part of the UCSD Advanced Research Experience, where they explored machine learning techniques like the perceptron algorithm.

We hope you enjoy playing our game and learning about cities!
