About Us

COASTLE is a coastal city classification tool built to predict whether a city is coastal based on weather data. This project was developed by two rising juniors in high school from San Diego, California. Arya Balenga and Ryan Sharifi developed this as a fun project during the summer of 2025 at the UCSD Advanced Research Experience to implement machine learning algorithms that they learned during their time at the internship, namely the perceptron. We hope you enjoy playing our game!
